Practice: Episode 1 📝

1) Listening Comprehension

1. Officer: "Varifrån kommer ni?"
A. Where are you going?
B. Where are you from?
2. AnAn: "Vi ska åka till Malmö."
A. We are going to Malmö.
B. We live in Malmö.
3. NiuNiu: "Finns det någon rabatt för barn?"
A. Is there a discount for adults?
B. Is there a discount for children?
4. NiuNiu: "Måste vi byta tåg?"
A. Do we have to change trains?
B. Do we have to buy tickets?
5. AnAn: "Titta, nu är vi i Sverige!"
A. Look, we are in Denmark now!
B. Look, we are in Sweden now!
