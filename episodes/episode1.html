🇸🇪 Episode 1: Hej Sweden!

AnAn and his sister NiuNiu have just landed at Arlanda Airport in Stockholm. Let's join them as they say their very first Swedish words!

The Story 📖

AnAn: Hej! Jag heter AnAn. 👋

(Hello! My name is AnAn.)

NiuNiu: Hej! Jag heter NiuNiu. 👧

(Hello! My name is NiuNiu.)

Airport Staff: Välkommen till Sverige! 🇸🇪

(Welcome to Sweden!)
