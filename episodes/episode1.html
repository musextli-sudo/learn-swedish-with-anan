🇸🇪 Episode 1: Hej Sweden!

AnAn and his sister NiuNiu have just landed at Arlanda Airport in Stockholm. Let's join them as they talk to the airport officer!

The Story 📖

💡 Click individual 🔊 for single lines, or the button above to hear the whole conversation!

Officer: Hej! Vad heter du? 🔊

(Hello! What is your name?)

AnAn: Jag heter AnAn. 🔊

(My name is AnAn.)

NiuNiu: Jag heter NiuNiu. 🔊

(My name is NiuNiu.)

Officer: Varifrån kommer ni? 🔊

(Where do you come from?)

AnAn: Vi kommer från Kina. 🔊

(We come from China.)

Officer: Välkomna till Sverige! 🔊

(Welcome to Sweden!)

AnAn: Tack! 🔊

(Thanks!)

Officer: Hej då! 🔊

(Goodbye!)
